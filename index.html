Silfra

Silfra is a freshwater dive site in Iceland located between the North American and Eurasian tectonic plates.

Glacier water is filtered through porous volcanic rock, making it some of the clearest water in the world

Nusa Lembongan

Nusa Lembongan is an island off the coast of Bali, Indonesia, that is home to several pelagic species such as the whale shark, mola mola and manta rays.
